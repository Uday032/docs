---
title: "Outbound Calls"
description: "SendCall is a powerful feature on the Svalync platform that allows you to manage outbound  seamlessly. This guide will walk you through the step-by-step process of setting up agents, sending calls, reviewing call logs."
icon: "phone-arrow-up-right"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Agent">
    Start by navigating to the Agents page and clicking on Create. Set up your agent by adding a name, welcome message, prompt, language, voice, and knowledge base. This agent will act as your virtual caller or receiver, tailored to your specific needs.
    <Frame>
      <img className="" src="/images/voiceai/sendcall/1.png" alt="sendcall" />
    </Frame>
    <Frame>
      <img className="" src="/images/voiceai/sendcall/2.png" alt="sendcall" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a Call">
   Once your agent is set up, go to the Send Call page. Here, you can add the phone number you wish to call and select the agent you created earlier. This ensures the call is handled according to the agent's configuration.
    <Frame>
      <img className="" src="/images/voiceai/sendcall/3.png" alt="sendcall" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Call Logs">
    After completing the call, head over to the Call Log page. This section provides a detailed overview of all calls made, including their status, duration, and other essential details.
    <Frame>
      <img className="" src="/images/voiceai/sendcall/4.png" alt="sendcall" />
    </Frame>
    </Step>
  <Step title="Access Call Details">
    Select a specific call log to view the Call Details page. Here, you can dive deeper into the call's specifics, such as transcripts, summaries, status, tokens used, start time, and end time. This information is invaluable for analyzing call performance and agent effectiveness.
    <Frame>
      <img className="" src="/images/voiceai/sendcall/5.png" alt="sendcall" />
    </Frame>
    </Step>

</Steps>
